# Cyberghost Extra Classes

Welcome to **Cyberghost Extra Classes**  a learning hub dedicated to mastering **Full Stack Development with Java**.  
Our mission is to equip learners with the skills and confidence to build modern, scalable, and efficient applications from front-end to back-end.

---

What We Teach

We cover the **complete full stack development journey** using Java as the backbone. The technologies you’ll learn include:

### Front-End
- **HTML5**  
- **CSS3**  
- **JavaScript & TypeScript**  
- **Bootstrap**  
- **Tailwind CSS**  
- **Angular**

### Back-End
- **Node.js**  
- **Spring Framework (Spring Boot, Spring Security, Spring Data JPA, etc.)**  
- **Java**

### Databases
- **MySQL**  
- **PostgreSQL**  
- **MongoDB**

---

##  How It Works

- Weekly **challenges** are posted every weekend.  
- Learners are encouraged to submit their **best and most efficient solutions**.  
- Submissions must include **clear documentation** explaining the approach, design decisions, and implementation details.  
- The **top solutions** will be recognized and shared with the community.

---

##  Our Goal

To create a collaborative environment where aspiring developers can:
- Gain hands-on experience with industry standard tools and frameworks.  
- Build a strong portfolio of projects and solutions.  
- Develop problem-solving and documentation skills essential for professional growth.  

---

## Get Involved

Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced coder looking to sharpen your skills, **Cyberghost Extra Classes** is the place to grow.  
Stay consistent, tackle challenges, and become a confident full stack developer.
